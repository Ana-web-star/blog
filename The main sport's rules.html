Race Format

  • Weekend Format: Includes practice sessions (usually 2-3), qualifying, and the race.

  • Qualifying: A knockout format determines starting grid positions.

  • Sprint Races: On some weekends, a short Saturday race (Sprint) also affects grid or points.

Car Regulations

  • Technical Specs: Strict limits on engine type (1.6L turbo-hybrid V6), aerodynamics, weight (min. ~798 kg), fuel usage, and materials.

  • Development Cap: Teams must design within a budget and aero testing limits.

Sporting Regulations

  • Point System: Top 10 finishers score points.

  • Penalties: Drivers can get time penalties, grid drops, or fines for breaking rules (like causing collisions or speeding in the pit lane).

  • Track Limits: Drivers must stay within track boundaries or face warnings/penalties.
