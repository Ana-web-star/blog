The 2024 Formula 1 season has been action-packed, with a range of talented drivers securing victories across the various races. Max Verstappen started strong, winning multiple races including the Bahrain, Saudi Arabian, Australian, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, and Canadian Grand Prix. His consistent performance has kept him at the top of the standings, reinforcing his dominance in the sport.

Other standout winners include Carlos Sainz Jr., who took his first win of the season at the Australian Grand Prix, and Lando Norris, who claimed victories in Miami and the Dutch Grand Prix. George Russell also made his mark by securing a win at the Austrian Grand Prix, while Lewis Hamilton ended his long winless streak with a victory at the British Grand Prix.

The season has also been a breakthrough year for Oscar Piastri, who took his first-ever F1 victory at the Hungarian Grand Prix. Meanwhile, Charles Leclerc earned wins at both the Monaco and Italian Grand Prix, adding to Ferrari's success during the year. The Singapore Grand Prix and the United States Grand Prix saw Lando Norris and Charles Leclerc as the winners, respectively, with Norris also claiming a win at the Qatar Grand Prix.
